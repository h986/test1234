Hex Bit Holder

I love my Chapman MFG hex bit set I got in my toolkit for gunsmithing school. I wanted a permanent holder for it on my desktop. I was working at a machineshop at the time and used a spare piece of .625"x4" stock to create this bit holder. I origincally designed one in Solidworks and printed it in Prusament Orange PETG using my Prusa Mini. The angled logo design was not going to work on a HAAS MiniMill, so my good friend Jack Routon redesigned it in Fusion 360 to have the same number of holes for bits and also retaining the cool hexagonal C logo.
